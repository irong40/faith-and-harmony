Commercial Marketing eight hundred fifty dollars (4K video, 3D model, raw footage, perpetual license). Inspection Data twelve hundred dollars (inspection grid photography, annotated report, exportable data).
  Add ons: Rush Premium is plus twenty five percent for 24 hour or plus fifty percent for same day. Raw File Buyout is plus two hundred fifty dollars. Brokerage Retainer is fifteen hundred dollars per month for five Listing Pro shoots.

Follow the writing constraints from CLAUDE.md: no dashes, no semicolons, no emojis, no asterisks, no colons in prose (colons in section headings are acceptable since this is a configuration prompt), active voice, short sentences.

The prompt should stay under 1500 words to keep LLM latency manageable for voice calls.
